Jurisdiction geometry data structure

Remark : Although the jurisdiction geography could have been integrated directly in the jurisdiction resource, this data can easily take several kilobytes or even megabytes for complex countries/states. Since the use case for the geography is mostly for lookup purpose and that many applications might need to access the jurisdiction to retrieve basic information, the geography has a dedicated resource.
