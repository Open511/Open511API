The open511 specification has been released as version 0.9. This version is considered "implementation ready" and will be used for development of the first applications. Any other jurisdiction wishing to implement Open511 should contact Open North. We also appreciate feedback and comments, so feel free to contribute (see the contribute section on the right menu).
